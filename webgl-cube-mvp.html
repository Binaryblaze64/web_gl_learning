WebGL Cube · MVP Pipeline

Each frame every cube corner travels through three matrices:
• Model — spins/positions the cube in the world
• View — moves the world relative to the camera
• Projection — adds perspective (far = smaller)

The GPU computes P · V · M · position for all 36 vertices in parallel, then fills 12 triangles with interpolated color.

Open the DevTools console (F12) to watch the matrices print.
